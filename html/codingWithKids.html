Coding with Kids

Position: Instructor

    General Responsibilities:
    • Create, understand, and document code in preparation for lessons with students
    • Read through and understand pre-made lesson plans
    • Instruct and inspire kids (ages 6-12) to learn how to program online and in-person
    • Be able to understand and debug projects made by students
    • Provide and communicate information requested by parents and guardians of students
    • Prep, clean, and maintain public facilities used for classrooms

If you would like to visit the Coding with Kids website, please click here

I tutored that occasional classmate during my time at the University of Alaska Anchorage, but Coding with Kids has significantly improved and cultivated my own teaching skills and style. Teaching and enriching the lives of young coders across the Country has given me a newfound respect for my own teachers from the past and joy knowing that my students will either remember the experience and/or knowledge I gave them for the rest of their lives.

Coding with Kids offers various program that help enrich and develop youth knowledge by offering a variety of curriculum through their program. Their main programs consist of week long coding camps, weekly continuous classes, and enrichment courses. I usually taught Camps with the occasional weekly class as well.

Arctic Valley Ski Area

Position: Ski Lift Operator

    General Responsibilities:
    • Operate regular ski lifts and t-bar
    • Operate a pull system for the tube park located at the bottom of the mountain
    • Communicate problems and relevant information about the mountainside to other workers and management
    • Help deliver (by ski) and hold equipment used to repair lifts and other mechanical systems
    • Put away and lock up equipment at the top and bottom of the mountain at the end of the day

I was employed at Arctic Valley Ski area for about 3 months during the beginning of 2019 when I was still pursuing my degree at the University of Alaska Anchorage. We had 3 lifts to get to the top of the mountain including a t-bar and I would rotate between them along with the other lift workers.

I had a lot of fun during my time at Arctic Valley. I carpooled and worked with friends from University while developing most of my skiing skills there. I will never forget the memories and unique experience.
